Navient Complaints Continue Amidst Lawsuit

By: Nina Branwell

Navient Corportation is one of the leading federal student loan services . A mounting number of lawsuits from teachers, numerous states' attorney and loan borrowers arounf the country. The lawsuit resulted in Navient having to pay a total of $1.85 billion in relief to private and public borrowers. This would equal about $260 dollars for each person.

There are a total of 2.6 million complaints against Navient. 1,974 of them happened within the last year. Most of the complaints come from student loans and credit reporting. The highest reported complaints monthly were in January 2022, at the start of the new year. During the spring, the from February to June, the complaints about Navient Corporation slow down.

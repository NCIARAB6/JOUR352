        The Page For All Your Go-To Information!

        Welcome to My Husky Fansite. Originally from Siberia, the Siberian Husky is a medium-sized working sled dog breed. Husikies have thick,double furred coats. Their ears are triangle-shaped and erect. They look very similar to the Alaskan Malamute, another dog breed.

      • The life span of a Siberian Husky ranges 12 to 15 years.

      • The color of their fur ranges from white, black, and gray, to incorporate shades of brown and even red! They can have different colored eyes as well.

      • Most Huskies are very intelligent and outgoing. This allows them to engage friendly and gentle behaviors. They are known to be very alert
